Admission: Privacy Policy for Bookless University Admission System

At Bookless University, we understand the importance of protecting your privacy and personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us when you use our admission system.

Information We Collect

When you use our admission system, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal information, such as your name, address, email address, phone number, date of birth, and social security number.
  • Academic information, such as your transcripts, test scores, and other application materials.
  • Other information you provide to us, such as your interests, activities, and employment history.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to us for the following purposes:

  • To process your application for admission to Bookless University.
  • To communicate with you about your application, including sending you updates on the status of your application and requesting additional information if needed.
  • To evaluate your eligibility for scholarships and other forms of financial aid.
  • To conduct research and analysis to improve our admission process and services.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your information seriously and have implemented technical, administrative, and physical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or use. We also limit access to your information to those who need it to perform their job functions, such as admission officers and IT staff.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your information to the following parties:

  • Third-party service providers who help us process your application and provide support services.
  • Government agencies or other organizations as required by law or regulation.
  • Other third parties with your consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we hold. You also have the right to object to or restrict the processing of your information, and to withdraw your consent at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact our admission office.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our admission process and applicable laws and regulations. We will notify you of any material changes to this policy by posting a notice on our website or by sending you an email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our admission process, please contact our admission office.

Refund Policy for Bookless University Admission Fees

At Bookless University, we understand that circumstances may arise that prevent you from attending the university after paying your admission fees. This refund policy outlines the conditions under which you may be eligible for a refund of your admission fees.

Registration fee Never refundable!!!

Refund Eligibility

If you withdraw your application for admission to Bookless University before the admission deadline, you may be eligible for a full refund of your admission fees. However, if you withdraw your application after the admission deadline, your admission fees will not be refunded.

If you are denied admission to Bookless University, you may be eligible for a refund of your admission fees, minus a processing fee. The processing fee will vary depending on the amount of the admission fees paid.

Refund Process

To request a refund of your admission fees, you must submit a written request to our admission office. Your request must include your full name, contact information, and a statement explaining why you are requesting a refund.

If your request is approved, we will process your refund within 30 days of receiving your request. Refunds will be made by the same method used to pay the admission fees, unless otherwise requested.

Exceptions

There may be certain circumstances where exceptions to this refund policy may apply. These include:

  • If you are unable to attend Bookless University due to a medical or personal emergency, we may consider your request for a refund on a case-by-case basis.
  • If Bookless University cancels your admission due to circumstances beyond your control, such as natural disasters or government-mandated closures, we will provide a full refund of your admission fees.

Changes to this Refund Policy

We may update this refund policy from time to time to reflect changes in our admission process and applicable laws and regulations. We will notify you of any material changes to this policy by posting a notice on our website or by sending you an email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this refund policy or our admission process, please contact our admission office.
